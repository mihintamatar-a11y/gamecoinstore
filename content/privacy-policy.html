?? Privacy Policy

GameCoin Store ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Player ID/Game User ID

  • Email address or contact number (if provided during checkout or support)

  • Payment details (handled securely through third-party gateways – we do not store card details)

  • IP address, browser type, and device information (for analytics and security)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for:

  • Processing top-up orders

  • Delivering game currency or items

  • Providing customer support

  • Fraud detection and prevention

  • Improving website performance and user experience

3. Payment Security

We use trusted third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal). Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences

  • Improve website performance

  • Track usage data anonymously via tools like Google Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third parties, except:

  • When required by law

  • To fulfill your order through payment gateways or game APIs

  • For fraud prevention services

6. Data Protection

Your data is protected through:

  • SSL encryption

  • Secure server architecture

  • Access control

7. User Rights

You can request:

  • Access to your stored information

  • Correction or deletion of your data

  • Opt-out of marketing communication (if applicable)

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
