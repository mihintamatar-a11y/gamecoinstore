MLBB x Spongebob: Release Date, Skins, Emote & All Event Rewards

The long-awaited MLBB x Spongebob collaboration is finally happening! The event officially begins on 28 November 2025, while the Spongebob skins will be available on 12 December 2025. Until then, players can complete missions and collect event coins to prepare for the skin draw.

MLBB x Spongebob: All Heroes & Skins

This crossover brings twocharacters from Bikini Bottom into the Land of Dawn, each with fun animations and underwater-themed effects.

Cyclops: Spongebob Squarepants

Cyclops transforms into Spongebob with his classic outfit—square pants, red tie, shiny shoes, pirate hat, and wooden sword. His skills get playful new animations filled with bubbles and jellyfish-like effects.

Gloo: Patrick Star

Patrick fits perfectly with Gloo’s quirky style. When Gloo splits apart, the pieces turn into cute pink Patrick-like blobs that stick to enemies, giving every fight a funny but dangerous twist.

MLBB x Spongebob Emote: Squidward Appears

Even though Squidward didn’t get a full skin, he still joins the event as an exclusive emote. His classic annoyed expressions are great for taunting or joking with teammates. A must-have for Squidward fans!

Other Event Cosmetics

The event also includes many themed cosmetics to complete your Bikini Bottom collection:

  • Avatar Border – underwater frame with Spongebob & Patrick

  • Lobby Banner – bright ocean-blue banner

  • Chat Bubble – Spongebob-themed pop-up messages

  • Killing Notification – jellyfish sting animations

  • Recall Effect – pirate ship animation

  • Elimination Effect – electric jellyfish zap

  • Spawn Effect – popping out of Spongebob’s pineapple house

Each item adds a fun underwater feel to MLBB matches.

Final Thoughts

The MLBB x Spongebob collaboration brings fun animations, colorful skins, and nostalgic characters into the game. Make sure to join the event and start collecting coins early for the best rewards.
