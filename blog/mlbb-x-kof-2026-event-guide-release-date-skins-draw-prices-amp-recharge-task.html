MLBB x KOF 2026: Schedule, Skins & Recharge Missions

The legendary MLBB x King of Fighters (KOF) collaboration is returning in 2026! If you missed the previous event, this is your chance to grab iconic KOF skins once again through the Lucky Draw and Resale Event.
 

MLBB x KOF 2026 Release Date

The event is to begin on January 30, 2026 till March 7 March, 2026
During this time, players can:

  • Participate in the KOF Lucky Draw

  • Earn tokens through recharge missions

  • Unlock exclusive KOF skins

  • Get Epic, Special, and Limited rewards
     

Available KOF Skins in 2026

This year, the event is to include nine KOF skins (more than previous years). Expected skins include:

  • Karina – Leona

  • Dyrroth – Orochi Chris

  • Aurora – Kula Diamond

  • Guinevere – Athena Asamiya

  • Gusion – K’

  • Valir – Kyo Kusanagi

  • Paquito – Terry Bogard

  • Masha – Mai Shiranui

  • Chou – Iori Yagami

These skins are returning through the resale event, giving players another chance to own them.
Note??: Players are guaranteed to get Karina “Leona” within the first 10 draws.
If you already own it, you will receive a random Epic Skin instead
 

MLBB x KOF 2026 Draw Prices

  • 1x Draw: 225 Diamonds (50% OFF first daily draw)

  • 10x Draw: 2,250 Diamonds

  • First 10x Draw Discount: 30% OFF → 1,575 Diamonds
     

How the KOF Draw System Works

The event uses two prize pools:

  • KOF Bingo Pool

  • KOF ’97 Pool

Players can switch between pools while keeping their Bingo progress. Completing Bingo lines guarantees a KOF skin or an Epic skin if already owned.
 

KOF Tokens & Recharge Missions

To reduce Diamond costs, Moonton usually provides KOF Tokens through Premium Supply recharge tasks.

  • 2 Phases of Recharge Events

  • Around 16 Tokens per phase (32 Tokens total)
     


Mission rewards:

  • Log in to game – 1 Token

  • Recharge any amount – 1 Token

  • Recharge 50 Diamonds – 1 Token

  • Recharge 100 Diamonds – 4 Tokens

  • Recharge 250 Diamonds – 5 Tokens

  • Spend 100 Diamonds – 2 Tokens

  • Spend 250 Diamonds – 2 Tokens
     

Tips to Save Diamonds

  • Use the 50% daily draw discount

  • Complete both recharge phases

  • Collect free tokens from missions

  • Plan your Bingo pattern carefully
     

Final Thoughts

The MLBB x KOF 2026 event is one of the most exciting collaborations of the year. With guaranteed rewards, token missions, and rare skins, it’s the perfect time to expand your collection.
